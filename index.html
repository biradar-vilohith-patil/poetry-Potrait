MAI EK ANJAAN
DEKH KAR TERI MUSKAAN
HAIRAAN RAH GAYA.

MY POV

I am a stranger to you, the first time I saw you, I was amazed. A girl living life on her terms with lots of joy.

CHAHE PHOOL HO YA CHAAND
TERI TULNA ME SANSAAR KAM PAD GAYA.

MY POV

Then maine kavitha likhne ko try kiya, Usually purane zamane se poets used to describe girls with moon and flowers. I tried comparing with something but Kuch bhi barabar ka nahi tha. There comes the second stanza.

TERE AANKHON MEIN PURA BRAHMAND SAMA GAYA
JABHI DEKHU DIL GARDEN GARDEN HO GAYA.

MY POV

You have captivating eyes. The eyes filled with joy and masti. So the third stanza is..

NANHE SE BAAL TERE
PYARI SI HAI TU.
RAJDHANI ME REHNE WALI RANI SI HAI TU.

MY POV

Your campus is IIT Delhi. You live in the capital i.e. RAJDHANI. So the line is RAJDHANI ME REHNE WALI RANI SI HAI TU.

GOL MATOL GAAL TERE
SHARARAT SI HAI TU
USS MATHE KI BINDI ME PURI RAAT SIMAT JAYE AISI EK APSARA HAI TU.

MY POV

This tells About the cute mischievous things you do. And about the cuteness of your bindi.

KYA PUCHU US CHAAND SE KI KAISI HAI TU
VO CHAAND JO PIGHAL GAYA.

BEHTI NADI SI CHANCHAL TU
TERE AANKHON MEIN MAIN DOOB GAYA.

VRINDAVAN KI GALIYAN NAHI TO BANARAS KE GANGA GHAT HI SAHI
TERE SAATH SANSAAR KE CHAKKAR LAGANE KA MAN KAR GAYA.

MY POV

This tells about your interest towards travelling. Aapko ghumna pasand hai naa?? , Mujhe bhi travel karna, pahad chadna bohot pasand hai.

RAATON KI NEEND JALAKAR
DIL AUR JAAN SAB LAGA KAR
LIKHI GAYI YEH KAVITA,
TERE MUSKAAN KE BINA JO THA ADHURA,
TERE PADHNE SE AB PURA HO GAYA.

MY POV

Ab itna kuch likhne ke baad bhi mujhe laga tha ki kuch na kuch toh missing hai. Toh woh kami Aapne yeh padh ke pura kar diya üòÅ, isi tarah se haste aur hasate rahiye Rathod ji , Aap haste hue bohot achhe lagte hai.
